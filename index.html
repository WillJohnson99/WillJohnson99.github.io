Mischievous Night

Mischievous Night is an upcoming modification by TOS Team member and founder: Will Johnson. It revolves around Bart, committing countless heinous offenses and pranks on the night called mischievous night. After last years events on the same night, Bart went to court and was advised to be locked in his room and grounded on the exact night the next year. The night has finally arrived and Bart is having none of this, he hatches a plan to cause havoc till the night is out!

  • Release Date: October 30th 2016

Jimmy Gibbs Jr. Racecar
