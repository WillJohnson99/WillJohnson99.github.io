About Us

TOS Team is a group of individuals who are passionate about modding The Simpsons: Hit And Run, and have been modding it for 2 years, we make tools, mods, and other content related to the game. We have developed a .mfk checker, which is in beta and almost ready for a release thanks to xUnknown, a main mod The Odyssey of Springfield, and various open-source car mods which can be found on Donut Team's forum.
